Star Wars

    A New Hope

    A New Hope came out in 1977, it was the first movie that George Lucas made and the first of the series, it was the start of many. The story takes place in a distant galaxy filled with many planets, species, and powerful heroes and villains. The main struggle is between the Jedi, who use the Force for good, and the Sith, who use the dark side. Characters like Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, Princess Leia, and Han Solo have become some of the most recognizable figures in pop culture. Over the years, Star Wars has grown into a huge franchise that includes movies, TV shows like The Mandalorian, books, comics, and video games, making its universe richer and more detailed.
