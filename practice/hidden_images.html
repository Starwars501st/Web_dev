Doctor Who 2005

Doctor Who first aired on BBC TV in the United Kingdom on November 23, 1963. It's about an alien who travels through time and space in a blue police box-shaped ship called the TARDIS. The Doctor can change appearance by regenerating, which is why different actors play the role. With the help of various companions, the Doctor visits new worlds, meets strange creatures, and works to keep the universe safe.

Doctor Who's Main/Most Known Companions

  • Sarah Jane Smith
  • Rose Tyler
  • Martha Jones
  • Donna Noble
  • Amy Pond
  • Rory Williams
  • Clara Oswald

Many of the Doctor's companions become important because they help the Doctor grow and change. Some, like Amy, Rory, and Clara, have stories that connect to the Doctor in big time-travel ways. Others, like Donna and Martha, show how traveling with the Doctor can make someone braver and stronger.
